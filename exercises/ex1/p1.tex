Implement a C/Fortran program which calculates
\[
  \underline{y} = \underline{A}\underline{x}
\]
where 
\[
  \underline{A} = 
    \begin{bmatrix}
      0.3 & 0.4 & 0.3 \\
      0.7 & 0.1 & 0.2 \\
      0.5 & 0.5 & 0.0
    \end{bmatrix},
    \underline{x} = \begin{bmatrix}
                      1.0 \\
                      1.0 \\
                      1.0
                    \end{bmatrix}.
\]
